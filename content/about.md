## Who We Are

Westside Youth Rugby is a community rugby club serving young athletes on Portland's Westside. We welcome players of all experience levels.

## Our Mission

We develop young rugby players in a safe, supportive environment that emphasizes teamwork, respect, sportsmanship, and fitness.

## Where We Play

Portland, Oregon. Practice and game locations are announced at the start of each season.

## Who Can Join

Youth players in the Portland metro area. No prior rugby experience required.
